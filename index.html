Do more with money together

#DoMoneyBetterTogether

Planly sets you and others up for money success. It lets you jointly plan and work towards a shared goal, automating all the finances, all while allowing you to learn from others that have achieved your goal before.

Collaborate with people you know.

When you’re doing money things together, use Planly to keep track of your discussions, dreams and plan. Make decisions and manage budgets together.

Get connected to expert advice.

Your goal may require some expert advice from someone like a financial advisor. Planly gives you access to these kinds of people, allowing you to share your goals and get the advice you need, without having to meet face to face. If you like, you can give your financial advisor access to Planly to comment, give input or even recommend edits to your plan.

Get tips and tricks from people who've done it before.

You don’t have to go it alone! Get tips and support from the community so you can manage money things better. Use logical plans and steps from people like you.
